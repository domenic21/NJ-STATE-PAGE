Trenton

Trenton is the capital city of the U.S. state of New Jersey and the county seat of Mercer County. It briefly served as the capital of the United States in 1784.

Atlantic City

Atlantic City is a resort city on New Jersey's Atlantic coast that's known for its many casinos, wide beaches and iconic Boardwalk. Established in the 1800s as a health resort, today the city is dotted with glitzy high-rise hotels and nightclubs.

Newark

Newark is a city in New Jersey, just west of New York City. It’s known for the Newark Museum, with its American and Tibetan collections, and its extensive collection of American Gothic art.
